Catfish

A catfish has about 100,000 taste buds, and their bodies are covered with them to help detect chemicals present in the water and also to respond to touch.

  • Scientific Name: Siluriformes
  • Average Length: 12 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 12 to 14 years
  • Habitat: Rivers, lakes, ponds, steams, swamps, etc..

Though these fish vary dramatically in size, color, and shape, you will likely recognize one when you see it.
They have several long, fleshy growths near their mouths, which look like cat whiskers, which researchers call “barbels.”
